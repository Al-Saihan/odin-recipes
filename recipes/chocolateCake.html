How to Make a Delicious Chocolate Cake!

Description:

Who doesn't like chocolate cakes? Right? Whether it's for a birthday or a special celebration, chocolate cakes always brighten everyone's mood!

Ingredients:

  • Sugar: 2 cups
  • All-purpose flour: 1 ¾ cups
  • Unsweetened cocoa powder: ¾ cup
  • Baking powder: 1 ½ teaspoons
  • Baking soda: 1 ½ teaspoons
  • Salt: 1 teaspoon
  • Eggs: 2 large
  • Milk: 1 cup
  • Vegetable oil: ½ cup
  • Vanilla extract: 2 teaspoons
  • Boiling water: 1 cup

Cooking Steps:

  1. Gather all the ingredients and preheat the oven to 350°F (175°C).
  2. Grease and flour two 9-inch round baking pans.
  3. In a large bowl, stir together sugar, flour, cocoa, baking powder, baking soda, and salt.
  4. Add eggs, milk, oil, and vanilla; mix for 2 minutes on medium speed with an electric mixer.
  5. Stir in the boiling water. The batter will be thin.
  6. Pour the batter evenly into the prepared pans.
  7. Bake in the preheated oven until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean, about 30 to 35 minutes. Cool in the pans for 10 minutes, then transfer to a wire rack to cool completely.
  8. Finally, after waiting long enough, eat and enjoy!
